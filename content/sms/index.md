# SMS Notifications
SMS Consent & Opt-Out

By providing your phone number, you agree to receive test and notification messages from Secret Mustache. Consent is obtained verbally.

Messages will be limited in number and focused on notifying you when the service you are interested in becomes available.

You may stop receiving messages at any time by:

	•	Emailing stop@secretmustache.com, or
	•	Verbally requesting that notifications be discontinued.

Message and data rates may apply.
